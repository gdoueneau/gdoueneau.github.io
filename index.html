Research

From 2020 to 2023, I did a PhD in computer science (more information here) under the supervision of Olivier Carton and Emmanuel Filiot.

My publications are available here. My research deals with simple programs named transducers. In this context, I focus on program optimization problems: given a program, I try to find a more "more efficient" program (which consumes e.g. less time or less memory) which has the same behavior.
